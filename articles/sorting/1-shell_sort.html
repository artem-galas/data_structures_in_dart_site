Shell Sort

The Shellsort is good for medium-sized arrays, perhaps up to a few thousand items, depending on the particular implementation. It’s not quite as fast as quicksort and other O(N*logN) sorts, so it’s not optimum for very large files. However, it’s much faster than the O(N^2) sorts like the selection sort and the insertion sort, and it’s very easy to implement.
