Towers

The Towers of Hanoi is an ancient puzzle consisting of a number of disks placed on three columns.

The disks all have different diameters and holes in the middle so they will fit over the columns.

All the disks start out on column A. The object of the puzzle is to transfer all the disks from column A to column C. Only one disk can be moved at a time, and no disk can be placed on a disk thatâ€™s smaller than itself
